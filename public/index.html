The World of Common Day

Tony Stark is the famous CEO of Stark Enterprises.

The Call to Adventure

Tony Stark gets captured by the Ten Rings (terrorists) and is forced to build missiles for them.

Refusal of the Call

He gets hurt and is kept alive by an arc reactor. The machine is running out of power, meaning he might die soon. He still refuses to build the missiles and would rather die.

Meeting with Mentor

Yinsen, a doctor brought in to care for Stark, urges him to find a way out, and builds a suit for Stark to escape with.

Crossing the First Threshold

Stark, using the newly made Iron Man suit, escapes the cave. However, Yinsen dies helping him get out. On the way out, Stark destroys the terrorist base.

Tests, Allies, and Enemies

Pepper Potts, Rhodey, and Obadiah (friends and colleagues of Stark) all tell him to stop working on the arc reactor and the suit, but he continues to proceed with development.

Approach to the Inmost Cave

He begins to build new suits with special features, including flight. He tests them multiple times, though the results are often dangerous. In the end, he finally overcomes these mistakes and builds a suit that is fully-functional and ready for use.

The Supreme Ordeal

Stark goes to Afghanistan with his suit to attempt to defeat Ten Rings. He accidently enters a no-fly-zone and is chased by fighter planes trying to shoot him down. He is able to save a pilot, though he risked his life for it.
